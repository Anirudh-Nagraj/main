Research & Accessibility

I have been involved in research on accessibility from the time I joined the university and I am currently investigating the navigational habits of people who are blind. I intend to look at ways in which a user-centered approach can increase accessibility. Through the data collected, I intend to look at ways in which participatory design can be incorporated with such an audience and set reference pointers for future projects related to accessibility and navigation in low-and middle income countries. My projects have taken me from blind schools to house-holds with acute water shortages to sitting on an office chair, working on company projects, to sitting in a classroom again ;only this time, I ponder on how to make people‚Äôs lives easier through better design through my efforts in the world of UX.
