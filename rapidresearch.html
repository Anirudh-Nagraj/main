Background and Role

Thus far, I have oprerated with two products - ONVIO and UltraTax. Onvio Firm Management combines document management, time and billing, trial balance capabilities, online client collaboration, and project management in a single integrated product. Takeaways

80 minute interviews are exhaustive and recruiting is a lenghty process. As a researcher, it is paramount to have enormous patience until the study is done. Qualitative coding helps in analyzing the findings. One key takeaway is each participant's view is different and can lead to different perspectives. Bias plays a very important role in the type of answer provided by the user.
