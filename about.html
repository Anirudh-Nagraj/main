Glad you stopped by! Hello once again! I am Anirudh - a soccer following, tea drinking, User Experience Researcher with an ever bleeding passion for accessibility, diversity and inclusion. I've worked in companies like UserWorks, Inc. and LTI. Currently at Reuters, I hold a graduate degree in Human-Centered Computing from the University of Maryland, Baltimore County.

Me and UX

By leveraging UX Research, I get into the users' minds whom businesses serve. I help identify the clients' and stakeholders' needs, thereby increasing the product's value at hand. I like to work with multiple methods (Interviews, Contextual Inquiries, Personas, etc.) to triangulate findings, backing up qualitative data with quantitative and vice-versa. The technique used in the research is dependent on the requirements of the businesses, stakeholders, and users. I am well versed in Assistive Technologies like JAWS, Dragon Naturally Speaking, NVDA. At Reuters, I work as the lead researcher on the rapid research program where we run qualitative studies on product features, workflows, and concepts for quicktime results and decisions to inform in a short period of time. I come with an engineering background and a deep rooted passion for inclusive design and accessibility. I employ various research methods and accessibility tools to help build impactful products, bringing customers and businesses closer to one another.
