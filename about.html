Bio

Glad you stopped by! Hello once again! I am Anirudh - a soccer loving, tea drinking, User Experience Researcher with an ever bleeding passion for accessibility, diversity, and inclusion. I've worked for companies like Grid Dynamics (Client - Google), Thomson Reuters, UserWorks, Inc. and LTI. I hold a graduate degree in Human-Centered Computing from the University of Maryland, Baltimore County.

Me and UX

By leveraging UX Research, I get into the users' minds whom businesses serve. I help identify the clients' and stakeholders' needs, thereby increasing the product's value.

I employ a mixed methods approach (viz., Context Interviews, Surveys, Benchmarking) and provide customer experience insights to businesses. With a specialized working knowledge of accessibility standards and tools (viz., JAWS, NVDA, ZoomText), I make the workplace, and products more inclusive and accessible.

I bring a unique blend of UX research experience from multiple domains and Accessibility, thereby helping businesses deliver products that cater to diverse audiences.
